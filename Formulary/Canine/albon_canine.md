# Drug Name: Albon

## Active Ingredient

Sulfadimethoxine

## Species

Canine

## Injectable Dose

- 55 mg/kg IV or SQ once, then 27.5 mg/kg q24h

## Dose

- 55 mg/kg PO once, then 27.5 mg/kg PO q24h

## Routes

PO, IV, SQ

## Notes

Albon is a sulfonamide antibiotic used primarily for the treatment of coccidiosis in dogs. It may also have activity against some bacterial pathogens.
