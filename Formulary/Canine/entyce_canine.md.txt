## Notes
Entyce (capromorelin) is a ghrelin receptor agonist used to stimulate appetite in dogs. It is FDA-approved for canine use and is especially helpful in managing inappetence due to chronic illness or post-operatively. Monitor for signs of vomiting or diarrhea. Not recommended for use in dogs with liver disease without veterinary oversight.
