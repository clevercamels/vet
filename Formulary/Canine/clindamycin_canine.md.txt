# Clindamycin - Canine

**Drug Name**: Clindamycin

## Active Ingredient
Clindamycin hydrochloride

## Species
Canine

## Oral Dose and Duration
- **Dental infections, osteomyelitis, wounds, abscesses:** 5.5–11 mg/kg PO q12h x 7–14 days  
- **Toxoplasmosis or protozoal infections:** 10–12 mg/kg PO q12h x 14–28 days

## Routes
PO

## Notes
Clindamycin is a lincosamide antibiotic effective against anaerobic bacteria and many Gram-positive organisms. Commonly used for dental infections, deep wounds, osteomyelitis, and protozoal infections like toxoplasmosis. Should not be used in animals with known hypersensitivity to lincosamides. May cause GI upset. Avoid in rabbits, guinea pigs, and horses due to risk of fatal colitis.
