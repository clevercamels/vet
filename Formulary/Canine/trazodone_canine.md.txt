# Trazodone - Canine

**Drug Name**: Trazodone

## Active Ingredient  
Trazodone hydrochloride

## Species  
Canine

## Injectable Dose  
N/A

## Oral Dose and Duration  
- **Anxiety, situational phobias, post-operative confinement:** 3–10 mg/kg PO q8–24h x as needed  
- **Adjunctive behavioral therapy:** 2–6 mg/kg PO q12h x ongoing

## Routes  
PO

## Notes  
Trazodone is a serotonin antagonist and reuptake inhibitor used for sedation and anxiolysis. Sedation is the most common side effect; paradoxical excitation can occur. May be combined with other anxiolytics or antidepressants but use caution with serotonergic drugs due to risk of serotonin syndrome.
