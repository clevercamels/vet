# Cefpodoxime - Canine

**Drug Name**: Cefpodoxime

## Active Ingredient
Cefpodoxime proxetil

## Species
Canine

## Injectable Dose
N/A

## Oral Dose and Duration
- **Skin and soft tissue infections (including pyoderma):** 5–10 mg/kg PO q24h x 7–14 days  
- **Urinary tract infections:** 5–10 mg/kg PO q24h x 10–14 days

## Routes
PO

## Notes
Cefpodoxime is a third-generation cephalosporin antibiotic commonly used in dogs for skin and urinary tract infections. It offers the convenience of once-daily dosing and has a broad spectrum of activity against Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria. It is generally well tolerated. Should not be used in animals with known hypersensitivity to cephalosporins or penicillins.
