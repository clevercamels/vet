# Prilosec - Canine

**Drug Name**: Prilosec

## Active Ingredient  
Omeprazole

## Species  
Canine

## Injectable Dose  
N/A

## Oral Dose and Duration  
- **Gastric ulcers, reflux esophagitis:** 0.7–1 mg/kg PO once daily x 1–4 weeks

## Routes  
PO

## Notes  
Omeprazole is a proton pump inhibitor used to treat gastric ulcers, reflux esophagitis, and other conditions requiring acid suppression. It is generally well tolerated but may cause GI upset or mild liver enzyme elevations. Duration of treatment should be based on clinical response and resolution of symptoms. Administer on an empty stomach for optimal absorption.
