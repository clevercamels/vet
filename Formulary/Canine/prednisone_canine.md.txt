# Prednisone - Canine

**Drug Name**: Prednisone

## Active Ingredient  
Prednisone

## Species  
Canine

## Injectable Dose  
N/A

## Oral Dose and Duration  
- **Anti-inflammatory:** 0.5–1 mg/kg PO BID x 3 days, then SID x 3 days, then EOD x 3 doses 
- **Immunosuppressive:** 2–4 mg/kg PO SID x 7–14 days, then taper as directed  
- **Physiologic replacement (Addison's):** 0.1–0.2 mg/kg PO SID until follow up.

## Routes  
PO

## Notes  
Prednisone is a synthetic corticosteroid used for anti-inflammatory, immunosuppressive, and replacement therapy. Chronic use may lead to polyuria, polydipsia, polyphagia, and iatrogenic Cushing's. Must be tapered gradually after prolonged administration. Use with caution in diabetic or infectious patients.
