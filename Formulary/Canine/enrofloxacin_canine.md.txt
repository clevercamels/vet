# Enrofloxacin - Canine

**Drug Name**: Enrofloxacin

## Active Ingredient
Enrofloxacin

## Species
Canine

## Injectable Dose
- **Urinary tract, skin, respiratory, GI infections:** 5 mg/kg IV or SQ q24h

## Oral Dose and Duration
- **Skin, respiratory, GI infections:** 5-10 mg/kg PO q24h x 7–14 days
- **Urinary tract infections:** 5 mg/kg PO q24h at night x 5 days

## Routes
PO, IV, SQ

## Notes
Enrofloxacin is a fluoroquinolone antibiotic with broad-spectrum activity against Gram-negative and some Gram-positive bacteria. Avoid use in young, growing dogs due to risk of cartilage damage. Use with caution in animals with seizures. Do not exceed 5 mg/kg in dogs over 8 years of age to minimize risk of retinal toxicity.
